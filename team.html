Let’s set you on a path to a healthier you.

A leading physical therapy provider, The Centre for Musculoskeletal Medicine is here to help you with all your treatment needs. Specialising in pain management and musculoskeletal injury treatment, we combine a unique mix of health care disciplines, offering a holistic approach to medicine. Our South Melbourne Osteopathy & Physiotherapy clinic is conveniently located. We’re helping the Albert Park, Middle & Port Melbourne communities move and feel better. We pride ourselves on accurate diagnosis and effective, gentle and affordable treatment.
